FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I know Quality Muncher is safe?

A: Since the file is completely open source, as seen in the GitHub, you can check all of the code yourself. If you have any questions about specific lines or sections, you can contact us and we'll answer any questions you may have.


Q: How do you install Quality Muncher manually?

A: You'll first need FFmpeg. Click here for a tutorial. After that, use the manual download button from the home page of this site, and type shell:sendto, and press enter. Drag Quality Muncher into that folder. Next, find the video you want to use, right-click it, then hover over "Send to" and click "Quality Muncher.bat" and follow the prompts it gives you.


Q: How do you use Quality Muncher?

A: Once installed, find the video you want to use, right-click it, hover over "Send to", then click "Quality Muncher.bat" and follow the prompts it gives you.


Q: What does Quality Muncher do?

A: Quality Muncher has a simple concept with many features. You can compress videos, make them look worse, add music, stretch a video, clip a video, modify the colors, and more, which you can find on the features page.


Q: Who are you?

A: Currently, Quality Muncher has two developers: Frost and Atzur.
