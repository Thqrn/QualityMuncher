FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do you install Quality Muncher manually?

A: First, use the download button from the home page, and type shell:sendto, and press enter. Then drag QualityMuncher.bat into that folder. Next, find the video you want to use, right-click it, then click QualityMuncher.bat and follow the prompts it gives you.


Q: How do you use Quality Muncher?

A: Once installed, find the video you want to use, right-click it, then click QualityMuncher.bat and follow the prompts it gives you.


Q: What does Quality Muncher do?

A: Quality Muncher has a simple concept with many features. You can compress videos, make them look worse, add music, stretch a video, clip a video, modify the colors, and more, which you can find on the features page.


Who are you?
