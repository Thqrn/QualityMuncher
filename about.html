Information about Quality Muncher

Quality Muncher, originally created by Frost/Thqrn, was made to compress, distort, or otherwise modify videos. It's written in batch and development started Mid-January of 2022 after Couleur gave Frost the idea of making a script that creates low quality videos, instead of creating them by hand.
